## Learning Path

For Advanced level, Tier 3 is integrated throughout the year:

| Months | Tier 3 Focus |
|--------|--------------|
| 1-3 | Foundation (minimal Tier 3) |
| 4-6 | MCP, A2A protocols, Kubernetes intro |
| 7-9 | Kafka, Spark, advanced deployment |
| 10-11 | ONNX, TensorRT, performance optimization |
| 12 | Advanced ML: Federated Learning, GNNs |

Tier 3 technologies typically require Tier 1 and Tier 2 as prerequisites.
